About

Policy analyst bridging research and impact across governance, technology, education, and international development in Sub-Saharan Africa and beyond. I work at the intersection of data, ethics, and strategic decision-making, from educational equity to energy transition to AI policy. Experience partnering with institutions like the Wilson Center, World Bank, and Johns Hopkins SAIS to translate complex analysis into strategic recommendations for diverse stakeholders: policymakers, foundations, private sector leaders, among others. This portfolio features my research, analysis, and policy work.
